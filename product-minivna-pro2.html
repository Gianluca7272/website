Why Pro 2 for HF/VHF?

Designed for 0.1–230 MHz, Pro 2 focuses on where most amateur, lab, and production work actually happens. Measuring at the feedpoint removes coax loss and ferrite fuss. The dual-channel I/Q DDS lets you run signal-gen experiments without extra boxes.

  • Pocket-size form factor; battery powered (~4 h)
  • Clean HF/VHF baselines; confident matching and filter work
  • Open-ended: integrate with fixtures, custom front-ends, or OEMs

Software & App

mRS Device Manager (Windows/Linux†) plus Android app for phone/tablet control.

  • Sweep S11/S21, full phase measurement, multi-trace overlays, markers & integrated Smith chart
  • Generator control (DDS), dual-port support, calibration manager (OSL)
  • Export in several formats: JPEG, Excel, ZPLOT, S2P, PDF

FAQ

Why cap at 230 MHz?
It’s intentional: Pro 2 specializes in HF/VHF where clean baselines and dynamic range matter most. If you need up to 3 GHz, pick miniVNA Tiny or ask us about a custom 2-Port VNA.
Do I need a phone or a PC to use it?
Yes — Pro 2 has no built-in screen. Use our Windows/Linux app or Android via Bluetooth. That’s what keeps the device pocket-size and affordable.
Can I run generator/IMD experiments from the app?
Yes. The integrated dual-channel I/Q DDS with 1° phase steps and 0–55 dB attenuation is controllable from the software.
