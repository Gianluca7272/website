Applications for VNAs

From antenna tuning to production QA, vector network analyzers quantify how RF networks reflect (S-parameters) so you can match, optimize, and validate quickly. Filter by context to see what fits your workflow.

Antenna Tuning & Matching

S11VSWRReturn-Loss

Measure feedpoint impedance at the antenna (not the bench) to eliminate feedline effects and hit target VSWR quickly.

Best with: miniVNA Pro² (field), 2-Port VNA (lab fixtures)
  • Live Smith chart + markers for rapid re-tune.
  • HF/VHF whips, wire, small log-periodics, loops, HF mobile.
  • Typical: 1–230 MHz; target RL < −10 dB (VSWR < 2:1).

RF Filters, Traps & Duplexers

S21S11BW/Q

Characterize passband ripple, insertion loss, group delay, and return-loss. Tune notch depth and skirt steepness.

Best with: 2-Port VNA (S21), optional fixtures
  • Measure S21 vs frequency to find −3 dB bandwidth and rejection.
  • Check matching (S11) to minimize reflections at ports.

Cables & Connectors

S11RLVelocity-factor

Verify return-loss, find damaged connectors, estimate cable length/velocity using phase slope.

Best with: miniVNA Pro² (field validation)
  • Open/short/load references for quick checks.
  • Good for site commissioning and maintenance.

Impedance Matching Networks

S11Smith

Design L/π networks for PA stages or antennas; place markers on Smith chart to hit target Γ or Z.

Best with: 2-Port VNA (bench), miniVNA Pro² (in-situ)
  • Synthesize values; verify real-world parasitics.
  • Iterate with live sweep and component swaps.

Transmission Lines & Baluns

S21Phase

Measure loss/phase per length, check balun balance and common-mode rejection, validate coax stubs.

Best with: 2-Port VNA
  • Derive velocity factor and effective εr.
  • Plot group delay vs frequency for dispersion.

SDR & RF Modules Bring-up

S11S21Phase

Verify front-end filters, LNAs, and matching on dev boards; confirm reference designs before layout freeze.

Best with: 2-Port VNA
  • Cal kits / de-embedding for accurate board-edge refs.
  • Sweep step size suitable for narrow filters.

Materials & Ferrite Evaluation

S21Loss

Compare cores, absorbers, and substrates via through-loss and phase change to choose cost-effective parts.

Best with: 2-Port VNA
  • Fixture repeatability dominates—keep lead dress identical.

Production QA / Go-No-Go

S11S21Limits

Automate acceptance windows (limit lines) for fast screening of assemblies, cables, and tuned products.

Best with: 2-Port VNA or miniVNA Tiny (compact rigs)
  • CSV export / APIs integrate with SPC dashboards.
  • Operator prompts and fixture interlocks recommended.
